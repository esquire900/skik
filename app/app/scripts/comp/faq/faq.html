What, why, how?

This webapp is made for the danish geek challenge, and as a small sideproject by Simon Nouwens. The idea is to give interested programmers a quick update on their preferred languages and allow them to hack the settings Sublime style.

How do I use it?

Basically add some languages in the languages page. Go back to the main page (click on the logo, top left) and see your feed of news and software appearing. Read articles on-site by clicking on them, or go to their original page. After fiddling around, go to settings and customize the app to your needs!
